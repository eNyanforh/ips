Science at the Service of People

The Institute for Population Studies is an independent, non-partisan research organization committed to generating and translating evidence on the dynamics of human populations ‚Äî how we live, move, age, and relate to our environments.

Our researchers partner with governments, international agencies, universities, and civil society to ensure demographic knowledge reaches those who most need it: policymakers, advocates, and communities.
